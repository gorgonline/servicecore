# ServiceCore Token Referansi

Gercek degerler icin `brand/tokens.json` ve `website/src/data/design-tokens.json` oku. Bu dosya sadece hizli basvuru.

## Renkler (tokens.json'dan)
- Primary: colors.brand.primary
- Secondary: colors.brand.secondary
- Accent: colors.brand.accent
- Surface Base: colors.surface.base
- Text Primary: colors.text.primary
- Text Secondary: colors.text.secondary

## Efektler (tokens.json'dan)
- Glass: effects.glass.base
- Glow: shadows.glow.primary
- Brand Gradient: effects.gradient.brandPrimary

## Boyut Disiplini
- Ikon/badge: w-8/h-8 asmasin
- Logo: w-12/h-12 asmasin
